<b>Tournant serves your favorite dishes on your Android device!</b>
With Tournant, you can manage your recipes for cooking and baking. You can sync the app with a recipe file from Gourmand Recipe Manager (previously known as Gourmet) or use Tournant as a standalone recipe manager.

<b>Features</b>
<ul>
<li>Create and edit recipes</li>
<li>Import und export XML files (Gourmand)</li>
<li>Sync with an XML file on the Android device</li>
<li>Search in recipes and categories</li>
<li>Share recipes</li>
<li>Scale ingredient lists</li>
</ul>
